Correlation Between Typing And Location Data

TD Typing Distance upper limit of typing - distances to be correlated
CD Contact Depth number of allowed intermediary contacts between two patients
0 = direct contact
MR Mutation Rate max. allowed typing addition for links to intermediate contacts
used for indirect contacts only (CD > 0)
e.g: Patients A & B with TypingDistance=3. MutationRate=2 allows for PatientLink of "A-X-B" to have "A-X" and "X-B" distance of up to 5.
