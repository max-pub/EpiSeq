Sample Parameters
Sgap Sample Day Gap allowed distance between sample-pairs in days
Contact Parameters
Cloc Contact Location Level select the adminstrative level of contacts that you want to be considered for correlation
Cgap Contact Day Gap specify the maximal temporal distance in days that you want to allow between contacts
(CT<0 = non-overlapping stays, the negative value indicates the days between stays)
(CT>0 = overlapping stays, the positive value indicates the days of overlap)
Intermediary Contact Parameters
Chop Contact Hops number of allowed intermediary contacts between two patients, 0 = direct contact
Ttol Typing Tolerance max. allowed additional typing distance for links to intermediate contacts
used for indirect contacts only (CD > 0)
e.g: Patients A & B with TypingDistance=3. MutationRate=2 allows for PatientLink of "A-X-B" to have "A-X" and "X-B" distance of up to 5.
